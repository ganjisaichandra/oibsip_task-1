A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

15 October 1931 - 27 July 2015

Missile Man of India

Resting Place:

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Desiya Ninaivagam, PeiKarumbu, Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India

Education Qualification:

Degree in Aeronautical Engineering from the Madras Institute of Technology (MIT)

Profession:

Sientist, Politician, Teacher, Author, Engineer, Administrator.

Awards:

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Award,Bharat Ratna Veer Savarkar Award, King Charles II Medal, International von Kármán Wings Award Hoover Medal, Indira Gandhi Award for National Integration, IEEE Honorary Membership, NSS Wernher Von Braun Memorial Award, Ramanujan Award.

“A Dream is not that which you see while sleeping is something that does not let you sleep.”
